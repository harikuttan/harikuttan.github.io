Project Name : Service Fusion
Duration : 6 months Team Size : 10
Technologies Used : YII framework,QBO,Angualr JS,Mysql.
Service Spot is a three component system consisting of a client facing mobile app, a field worker mobile app and an administration system. Clients will use the mobile app to find service providers in their area (e.g. plumber). They will be able to book service appointments with and request estimates from them. Clients will also be able to view their account history, view jobs in progress, view service technician’s location on the map and a lot more. The field worker app is designed for the service technician and will allow him to view and manage work orders, update work order statuses, clock in and out for work . The admin system will be used by the service company’s in office or mobile staff to manage their daily workload. They will be able to manage their company information, their teams, work orders/jobs, view field workers’ locations on the map (via GPS coordinates communicated by the worker mobile app or hardware GPS tracking devices in vehicles), run reports and more

Project Name :PaiGow Poker
Duration : 2 months Team Size : 4
Technologies Used : Cocos2D JavaScript framework.
Poker is a family of card games involving betting and individual play, whereby the winner is determined by the ranks and combinations of their cards, some of which remain hidden until the end of the game. Poker games vary in the number of cards dealt, the number of shared cards and the number of cards that remain hidden. The betting procedures vary among different poker games. Pai gow poker (also called double-hand poker) is an Americanized version of Pai gow (a Chinesegambling game). The game is played with a standard 52-card deck, plus a single joker.
